Welcome to My Blog

Hi, I am Ali, a chemical engineer data analyst with a strong interdisciplinary background in system process engineering, soft matter, and statistical design and analysis. I am passionate about working with organizations that want to make effective use of their data, from the early stage of Design of Experiments (DoE) for informative data collection, through process modeling, machine learning, and data visualization all the way to the impactful presentation of insights. I can support each step of the process to enable your organization to make evidence-driven decisions or share insights with new audiences.
Currently working as a postdoctoral researcher at The University of Texas at Austin. My research focuses on the mathematical modeling of soft-sticky particles, which leads to the development of new materials with a wide variety of applications. I am investigating the influence of an adhesion attraction force on the rheological behavior of sticky colloidal suspensions.
